Executive Director

Keith Ang'ana

A full-time tourist and a part-time Quantity Surveying student
at the University of Nairobi, I usually write on Philosophy, Science and Art
in a way that will be interesting to everyone. Besides that, I'm also looking
into creating Sheng' literature. Find me inside a cave or under a waterfall
learning the new lingo.
